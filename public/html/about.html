Who exactly are you guys?

We're just some college students trying to help reduce food waste and help with hunger and cost problems.

What exactly is Feed Me?

Other than being a quick hackathon project, the app inspires business owners to keep whatever leftovers they have so that non-profits and even lower-income individuals can come and take them at lower cost. Given that businesses throw away tons of food every year, and there are people who want that food, we hope to be the platform that bridges the gap between these two parties.

Why are you guys doing this?

Given the growing population in the states as well as agriculture challenges in producing states like California, we can't afford to keep throwing away perfectly good food or ingredients.
